Ras Mohammed

Ras Mohamed is located at Gulf of Aqaba on an area of 480 square kilometers.Due to Ras Mohamed’s geographical position, divers find almost permanent strong currents during all the year, which attracts larger fish. Beautiful beaches, extraordinary coral reefs and exciting dive sites make Ras Mohamed National Park a worthwhile visit.
